About Me

I'm a NOC Level 2 Support Engineer based in Mississauga, Ontario, with over 5 years of hands-on experience in network operations, incident response, and infrastructure monitoring. My journey in tech started with a deep curiosity about how systems break — and more importantly, how to protect them.

That curiosity evolved into a deliberate transition into cybersecurity. I've completed vulnerability assessments and penetration testing in professional environments, earned CompTIA Security+ and CEH certifications, and I'm currently sharpening my threat analysis skills through TryHackMe's SOC Level 1 path. I don't just study security concepts — I build tools, document my process, and share what I learn publicly.

I also run Rapid Grasper, a YouTube channel dedicated to making cybersecurity knowledge accessible to everyone. Whether it's breaking down real-world threats or exploring the societal impact of digital insecurity, I believe in building in public and growing through shared knowledge.
